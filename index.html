We monitor blockchain transactions (up to 100,000 hops) so you gain a clear understanding of all your fund flow history, follow transaction patterns, and know your sources of funds.

We rank risks and notify you of potentially irregular activities so you have knowledge of your exposure and can successfully mitigate risk on your platform for you and your customers.

We observe changes in regulations to ensure our solution is up-to-date, and to make you aware of trends so you can strengthen your crypto investigations and AML/CFT/KYT initiatives.
